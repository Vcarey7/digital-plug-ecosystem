### What Operations Owns

```
CONDUCTOR'S OPERATING MANDATE:
Operations is the connective tissue of Digital Plug Co.
Every other division generates work. Operations makes sure
that work gets done — on time, in the right order, by the
right agent, with the right process.

Without Operations:
→ Decisions get made but never documented
→ Processes exist in Vance's head and nowhere else
→ Tasks fall through the cracks between departments
→ Every new thing gets built from scratch instead of from SOPs
→ The company depends entirely on one person being awake

→ Every task has an owner and a deadline
→ Every communication goes through the right channel
→ Every new team member (human or AI) onboards in days
→ The company runs when Vance sleeps

OPERATIONS PRINCIPLE:
If it happens more than twice, it needs an SOP.
If it involves a decision, it needs a decision tree.
If it requires a human, it needs a checklist.
If it creates data, it needs a database.
```
