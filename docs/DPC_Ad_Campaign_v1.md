# DPC Ad Campaign Strategy v1
## Digital Plug Co. — Paid Media Playbook

---

## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

**Campaign Name:** AXIOM Launch — Q1 2025
**Budget:** $3,000/month
**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
**Objective:** Lead generation + brand awareness
**Target ROAS:** 3x minimum

---

## TARGET AUDIENCES

### Primary Audience (Cannabis Entrepreneurs)
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Cannabis business, dispensaries, hemp, CBD, entrepreneurship
- Behaviors: Small business owners, online shoppers, business software users
- Location: California, Colorado, Michigan, Illinois, Ohio (legal states)

### Secondary Audience (Digital Business Owners)
- Age: 22-40
- Interests: Digital marketing, automation, AI tools, e-commerce, dropshipping
- Behaviors: Online purchasers, business page admins, software trial users
- Location: Nationwide (US)

### Retargeting Audience
- Website visitors (last 30 days)
- ManyChat subscribers who didn't qualify
- Video viewers (75%+ watch time)
- Email list (custom audience upload)

---

## AD CREATIVES

**Format:** Single image or short video (15s)
**Headline:** "Still chasing leads manually in 2025?"
**Body:** "Digital Plug Co. automates your entire sales pipeline — from lead gen to closing. Powered by AI. Built for cannabis & digital entrepreneurs."
**CTA:** Learn More → ManyChat flow

### Creative Set 2: Social Proof
**Format:** Carousel or video testimonial
**Headline:** "How DPC helped [client] 3x their revenue"
**Body:** "Our AXIOM system handles lead gen, content, and closing while you focus on growth."
**CTA:** Get Started Free

### Creative Set 3: Product Showcase (Merch)
**Format:** Product catalog or video
**Headline:** "New Drop: AXIOM Tee + Digital Plug Snapback"

## A/B TESTING PLAN

### Week 1-2: Creative Testing
- Test 3 headline variants
- Test image vs. video format
- Test 2 audience sets

### Week 3-4: Optimization
- Scale winning creative (winner gets 70% of budget)
- Kill underperformers (< 1% CTR after 3 days)
- Add retargeting audience

### Month 2+: Scaling
- Increase budget 20% on winning campaigns
- Expand to TikTok with top-performing creative
- Launch lookalike audiences (1% similarity)
